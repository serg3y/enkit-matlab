Inputs:
wholesale_rrp_dollars_per_mwh[t] = AEMO recommended retail price ($/MWh) at interval t
amber_price_markup_factor = Amber's retail energy price markup costs, 1.1 (coef)
customer_usage_kwh[t] = customer consumption (kWh) at interval t
customer_export_kwh[t] = customer export (kWh) at interval t
distribution_loss_factor
distribution_loss_factor = network loss multiplier, â‰¥1 (coeficient)
amber_margin_cents_per_kwh[t] = additional retailer margin (cents per kWh)
network_charge_cents_per_kwh[t] = SAPN network charge (cents per kWh) at interval t
network_charge_cents_per_day = SAPN network daily charge (cents per day)
amber_charge_cents_per_day = standing charge per day (cents per day)
billing_days = number of days in the billing period
export_credit_cents_per_kwh[t] = feed-in tariff or solar sponge credit (cents per kWh)
gst_rate = 0.10

Equations:
1. Amber spot price (ex GST)
amber_spot_price_cents_per_kwh[t] = wholesale_rrp_dollars_per_mwh[t] / 10 * amber_price_markup_factor 

2. Amber energy price:
amber_energy_price_cents_per_kwh[t] = amber_spot_price_cents_per_kwh[t] * distribution_loss_factor + amber_margin_cents_per_kwh[t]

3. Total energy and network charge:
total_energy_and_network_charge_cents = sum( customer_usage_kwh[t] * (amber_energy_price_cents_per_kwh[t] + network_charge_cents_per_kwh[t]))

4. Total export credit:
total_export_credit_cents = sum( customer_export_kwh[t] * export_credit_cents_per_kwh[t] )

5. Subtotal (ex GST):
subtotal_before_gst_cents = total_energy_and_network_charge_cents - total_export_credit_cents + (network_charge_cents_per_day + amber_charge_cents_per_day) * billing_days 

6. GST amount:
gst_amount_cents = gst_rate * subtotal_before_gst_cents

7. Predicted total bill (inc GST):
predicted_bill_cents = subtotal_before_gst_cents + gst_amount_cents
